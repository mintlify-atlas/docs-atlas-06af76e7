# Welcome to STRIDE GPT

STRIDE GPT is an AI-powered threat modeling tool that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate comprehensive threat models and attack trees based on the STRIDE methodology. Whether you're securing a web application, API, or cutting-edge AI system, STRIDE GPT helps you identify security risks early in the development lifecycle.

## What is STRIDE GPT?

STRIDE GPT transforms the traditional threat modeling process by combining the proven STRIDE framework with advanced AI capabilities. You provide details about your application—type, authentication methods, data sensitivity—and the AI generates a detailed threat analysis tailored to your specific context.

## Specialized AI Application Support

STRIDE GPT includes cutting-edge support for modern AI systems:

- **Generative AI Applications**: Integrated OWASP LLM Top 10 threat categories
- **Agentic AI Systems**: OWASP ASI Top 10 for autonomous agent threats
- **Architectural Pattern Detection**: Automatically identifies RAG pipelines, multi-agent systems, code execution environments, and more
- **Cross-Layer Analysis**: Maps attack chains across architectural components

<Info>
STRIDE GPT is inspired by the [Cloud Security Alliance MAESTRO framework](https://cloudsecurityalliance.org/blog/2025/02/06/agentic-ai-threat-modeling-framework-maestro) for agentic AI threat modeling.
</Info>

## Flexible Model Provider Support

Choose the LLM provider that fits your needs:

- **Cloud Providers**: OpenAI (GPT-5 series), Anthropic (Claude 4.5), Google (Gemini 3), Mistral (Magistral series), Groq
- **Local Hosting**: Ollama and LM Studio for on-premises deployment
- **Advanced Reasoning**: Support for extended thinking modes across providers

## No Data Storage

Your security is our priority. STRIDE GPT does not store any application details or generated threat models. All data remains in your session and can be downloaded in Markdown format.
